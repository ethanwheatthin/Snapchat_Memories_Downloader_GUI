# Snapchat Memories Downloader GUI

A user-friendly desktop application to download and preserve your Snapchat memories with their original metadata, including dates and location information.

**Performance Tip:** For optimal processing of videos and captions, having VLC Media Player and FFmpeg installed on your system is highly recommended. VLC assists in smooth video playback and format conversion, while FFmpeg enhances video overlay merging. Download them here:

- [VLC Media Player](https://www.videolan.org/)
- [FFmpeg](https://www.ffmpeg.org/download.html)
